This Privacy Policy explains how BetSage ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you use our website and services.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type

We use browser local storage to save:

  • User preferences and settings

  • Session information

Note: This data is stored locally on your device and can be cleared at any time.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Send you important updates and notifications

6. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: HTTPS encryption for data transmission

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention
