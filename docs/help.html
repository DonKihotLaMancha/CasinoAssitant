üîß Troubleshooting

Common Issues:

Q: Cards not selecting in Poker?

A: Make sure you haven't already selected 2 cards. Click selected cards to deselect them first.

Q: Wheel not spinning?

A: Refresh the page and try again. Make sure JavaScript is enabled in your browser.

Q: Statistics not updating?

A: Statistics are session-based. They reset when you close the browser tab. This is by design for privacy.

Q: Results not showing?

A: Ensure all required fields are filled in before clicking Calculate. Check for error messages.

üìà Understanding the Results

Key Metrics Explained:

  • Win Probability: The percentage chance of winning the bet/hand
  • House Edge: The casino's mathematical advantage (lower is better for you)
  • Expected Value (EV): Average profit/loss per bet over time
  • RTP (Return to Player): Percentage of money returned to players over time
  • Volatility: How much results vary (high = big swings, low = steady)
üéØ Pro Tip: A negative expected value means you'll lose money over time. Focus on games/bets with the smallest house edge to minimize losses.
