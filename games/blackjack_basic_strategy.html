üí° Key Strategy Tips

  • Always split Aces and 8s - These are the most profitable splits
  • Never split 5s or 10s - Play them as hard totals instead
  • Double on 11 against dealer 2-10 - Highest expected value play
  • Stand on 17 or higher - Risk of busting is too high
  • Hit on 12-16 vs dealer 7-Ace - Dealer likely has strong hand
  • Double soft 13-18 vs dealer 5-6 - Dealer most likely to bust
  • Surrender 16 vs dealer 9, 10, A - If surrender is allowed
  • Never take insurance - House edge is too high
