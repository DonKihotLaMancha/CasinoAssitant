Starting Hand Strength Guide

Hand Type Examples Strength Action
Premium Pairs AA, KK, QQ Premium Raise/Re-raise always
Big Pairs JJ, TT, 99 Strong Raise most positions
Big Aces AK, AQ, AJ Strong Raise/Call
Medium Pairs 88, 77, 66 Good Call/Raise late position
Suited Connectors JTs, T9s, 98s Good Call in position
Small Pairs 55, 44, 33, 22 Marginal Call to set mine
Weak Aces A9, A8, A7 Marginal Fold early, call late
Trash Hands 72o, 83o, J4o Weak Fold always

üí° Key Tips

  • Position matters: Play tighter in early position, looser in late
  • Suited is better: Suited hands have 3% better equity
  • Pocket pairs: Set mining requires 15:1 implied odds
  • Ace-King: Strong but needs to improve post-flop
  • Suited connectors: Great for multiway pots
  • Avoid weak aces: Dominated too often
