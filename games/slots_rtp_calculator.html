üí° Understanding RTP

  • RTP = Return to Player - Percentage returned over time
  • House Edge = 100% - RTP - Casino's advantage
  • Long-term average - Not guaranteed per session
  • Higher RTP = Better odds - Always choose higher RTP
  • Online slots usually better - 95-97% vs 88-92% land-based
  • Volatility matters too - High volatility = bigger swings
  • Check the paytable - RTP should be disclosed
  • Progressive jackpots - Often have lower base RTP
